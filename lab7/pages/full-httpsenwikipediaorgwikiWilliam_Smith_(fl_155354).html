William Smith (fl. 1553/54)

William Smith (fl. 1553/1554) was an English politician.

Life[edit]

Due to the popularity of his name, Smith has not been identified.

Career[edit]

He was a Member (MP) of the Parliament of England for Newport, Cornwall October 1553 and Chippenham in April 1554.[1]

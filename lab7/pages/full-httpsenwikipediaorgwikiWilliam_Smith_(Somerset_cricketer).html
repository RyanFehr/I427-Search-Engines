William Smith (Somerset cricketer)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

William Robert Rutherford Smith (23 April 1871 – 23 December 1946) played first-class cricket for Somerset between 1895 and 1898 and for the MCC in 1901 and 1902.[1] He also played Minor Counties cricket for Wiltshire from 1899 to 1913 and for Worcestershire in minor matches before Worcestershire achieved first-class status in 1899. And he stood as an umpire in first-class cricket matches between 1919 and 1930. He was born at Batley, West Yorkshire and died at Ewell, Surrey.

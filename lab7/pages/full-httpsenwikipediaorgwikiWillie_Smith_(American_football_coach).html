Willie Sherrod Smith Jr. (born November 30, 1931) is a former American football coach. He served as the head football coach at North Carolina Central University in Durham, North Carolina and he held that position for four seasons, from 1973 until 1976. His coaching record at North Carolina Central was 25–15–3.[1]
